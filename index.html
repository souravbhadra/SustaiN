About

SustaiN is an effort to enable efficient nitrogen (N) management in corn and sorghum. It helps farmers to know how much inseason N is required to maximize yield and minimize N loss. Such practice in a larger region will ensure sustainable crop production practices and improved farmer profitability.

SustaiN is a "2022 North Central Graduate Student Grant" funded by NCR-SARE.
